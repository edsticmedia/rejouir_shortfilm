// About the Festival

A Celebration of
Independent Cinema

REJOUIR Short Film Festival is an annual celebration dedicated to the power of short-form storytelling. Born from a love of independent cinema, the festival provides a platform for emerging filmmakers to showcase their vision, craft, and voice to audiences who matter.

We believe every story deserves to be seen. REJOUIR invites college filmmakers from across the country to step behind the lens, challenge conventions, and create work that lingers long after the lights come up.

From intimate dramas to surreal vignettes — every genre, every language, every perspective is welcome here.

// Rules & Eligibility

Know Before You Shoot

01
College Students Only

The festival is open exclusively to currently enrolled undergraduate or postgraduate college students. Valid student ID will be required during registration.

02
Inter-College Teams Welcome

Teams may include members from different colleges. Collaborative spirit is celebrated here — cross-campus creativity is strongly encouraged.

03
Maximum 15 Minutes

Films must not exceed 15 minutes in total runtime, including title cards and credits. Films exceeding the limit will be disqualified from judging.

04
Open Theme

No prescribed theme. Tell the story you want to tell. Every genre, format, and narrative structure is welcome — experimental, documentary, fiction.

05
Original Films Only

Submitted films must be original works created by the submitting team. No adaptations of copyrighted material without written permission. Plagiarism results in immediate disqualification.

06
One Entry Per Team

Each team may submit only one film. However, individual members may be part of separate teams with distinct films. Quality over quantity.

// Submission Guidelines

What We Need From You

  • 01
    Film Title
    The official title of your short film as it should appear in all festival materials and credits.
  • 02
    Director Name(s)
    Full name(s) of the director(s). This will be used for award certificates and all official communications.
  • 03
    College Name
    The name of your institution as registered with your university or affiliation board.
  • 04
    Film Synopsis
    A compelling synopsis of no more than 150 words that captures the soul of your film — no spoilers required.
  • 05
    Private YouTube Link
    Upload your film as an unlisted video on YouTube and submit the link. Do not make the film public before the screening announcement.
